    About

    Disney Death Tour is not a parody of Walt Disney World or commercial tourism. It is an alternative writing of and in the Disney Corporation's carefully mediated spaces, but it is not graffiti. Disney Death Tour is a MEmorial, a digital means of interfacing with shared community values. We take very seriously the role of death in Disney, and hope to make visible entertainment's often overlooked consequences.
